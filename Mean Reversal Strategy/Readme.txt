This stratgy of mean reversal is the backtest on the nifty50.  
* Long Condition:
                  when the ratio of current price with respect to sma 20 is less than 5 percent of its current price.  
* Short Condition:
                  when the ratio of current price with respect to sma 20 is greater than 95 percent of its current price.
Accuracy for this strategy is nearly 90%.
